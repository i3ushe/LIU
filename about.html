About LIU Mauritania

Excellence in Education | Global Presence | Local Impact

Introduction

Lebanese International University is a premier institution of higher education offering world-class programs in Science, Technology, Business, and Languages. Located in Nouakchott, Mauritania, we provide comprehensive education following the LMD system with 6 undergraduate programs.

Founder & President

Mr. Abdul Rahim Mourad

Founder and President of LIU Mauritania

Mr. Abdul Rahim Mourad established Lebanese International University with the vision of providing quality education that bridges Lebanese educational excellence with international standards. His commitment to academic rigor and student development has shaped LIU into a respected institution.

Our Mission

To provide world-class education that prepares students for success in their professional careers and contributes to the development of society.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution of higher education recognized for academic excellence, innovation, and contribution to global knowledge.
