Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application deadline?

Applications are accepted on a rolling basis until all spaces are filled. We recommend applying early to secure your place.

When does the semester start?

The academic year starts in September each year. Classes run from September to June.

Is financial aid available?

Yes, various scholarships and financial aid packages are available for qualified students. Contact our admissions office for more information.
